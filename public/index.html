How to Guide

How do I connect my wallet

Please use Metamask to connect your wallet.

How many tokens will I receive?

Rewards are dependent on the number of memes submitted. The rewards will decrease over time as more and more memes are submitted to the vault.

Why is there a submission fee?

100% of the fees are collected and redistributed to MMV stakers. This is our way of compensating our contributors.  

What type of images are accepted?

Images such as .jpeg, .jpg and .png are acceptable. Also, .gif are good as well.

Size limited is 1MB.

Can I post multiple memes?

Yes and no. The first 230 memes will have unique owners. After that, you can submit one meme every 23 vaults. 

Example: If you submitted a meme to Vault #300, you will have to wait until there are 23 more memes submitted by other users before you can submit again.

Can I submit the same meme twice?

No! And don't you dare try it. We will know and reject it.
